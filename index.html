Our Story

Lypach Investment Ltd was founded by Francis Waweru Mukuria with a vision to revolutionize land investment in Kenya. Having witnessed countless Kenyans lose money to fraudulent land deals, Francis established Lypach to provide a secure, transparent platform for land verification and investment.

Our company combines traditional land brokerage with modern investment strategies, offering both individual land purchases and collective investment opportunities through our innovative Investment Club model.
